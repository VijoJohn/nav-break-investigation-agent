NAV validation ensures that the Net Asset Value of a fund reflects accurate valuation of securities and liabilities.

Key NAV validation checks include:

1. Security prices must be sourced from approved pricing vendors.
2. Price variance above tolerance levels must trigger investigation.
3. Income accruals must align with coupon schedules and dividend declarations.
4. Expense accruals must follow management agreements and contractual fee rates.
5. Distribution accruals should not exceed available income.
6. Position quantities must reconcile with custody records.
