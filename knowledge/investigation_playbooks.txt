NAV Break Investigation Playbook

Common drivers of NAV discrepancies include:

1. Price variance
   Security prices may differ from prior day pricing due to market movement or stale vendor feeds.

2. Income accrual mismatch
   Income accruals may exceed expected values if coupon calculations or dividend entitlements are incorrect.

3. Expense accrual variance
   Expense accruals may differ from expected calculations due to incorrect fee rates or NAV base values.

4. Distribution accrual discrepancy
   Distribution accruals may exceed available income if declaration calculations are incorrect.

5. Position quantity mismatch
   Differences between custody positions and accounting records may cause incorrect valuation.

Investigation Steps

1. Verify security pricing sources.
2. Recalculate accruals using contractual fee or coupon rates.
3. Compare recorded values with expected calculations.
4. Review historical investigations for similar patterns.
5. Escalate unresolved breaks to fund accounting oversight teams.
